From the Author

Welcome to my online book on contemporary abstract art! I am thrilled to have the opportunity to share with you my collection of abstract artworks, each one a unique and thought-provoking expression of my artistic vision.

As the creator and owner of these works, I have had the privilege of exploring the endless possibilities of abstract art, and of expressing my emotions, ideas, and experiences through color, line, shape, and texture. Whether through bold brushstrokes, subtle gradations, or intricate patterns, my artworks invite you to delve into their depths and discover their hidden meanings and emotions.

Contemporary abstract art is a vibrant and constantly evolving field, and I have always been drawn to its ability to challenge our preconceived notions of what art is, and to express ideas and emotions in a way that is both universal and deeply personal. My artworks are a testament to this power and potential, and I hope that they will inspire you to look at abstract art in a new light, and to appreciate its beauty and significance.

In this web page, you will find a selection of my most recent abstract artworks, created during the year 2022. Each one is a unique and highly personal expression of my artistic vision, and I hope that they will speak to you on a deep and meaningful level. Thank you for joining me on this journey of artistic exploration and expression, and I hope that you will find as much joy and inspiration in these works as I have.

- Karen
